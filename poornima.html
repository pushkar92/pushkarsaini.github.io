MOTIVATION

Motivation is a driving force that makes a human into action to achieve something. Motivation can be of two types :- 1. Internal -> is the motivation which is provided from a person's own mind. 2. External -> is the motivation provided from outside by other person.

If a person want to achieve something badly he can be motivated by his desire. It is the will of the person that force that to do something for his life.
